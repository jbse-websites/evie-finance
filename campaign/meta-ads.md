# EV Now — Meta / Instagram Ad Creative
## Ready to paste into Meta Business Manager

---

## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

**Campaign objective:** Leads (Lead Generation) or Conversions (if pixel is tracking form submits)
**Campaign budget:** Start at $30–50 NZD/day, optimise after 5–7 days
**Campaign name:** EVNOW_NZ_LeadGen_[Month][Year]

**Ad Sets (run 2–3 simultaneously, let Meta optimise):**
- Ad Set A: Cold audience — EV interest targeting
- Ad Set B: Warm audience — website visitors (retargeting, pixel required)
- Ad Set C: Lookalike — based on existing lead list (once you have 100+ leads)

---

## TARGETING — AD SET A (Cold)

**Location:** New Zealand
**Age:** 28–60
**Detailed targeting (stack these):**
- Electric vehicles
- Tesla (brand interest)
- Sustainable transport
- NZ Autocar (page engagement)
- EVs & Beyond (page engagement)
- Household income: top 25% (NZ)
- Auto buyers (Facebook data)
- Finance & investment interest

**Placements:** Automatic (Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels)
**Exclude:** People who have already submitted a lead (custom audience from pixel)
